Concept: Prime numbers are natural numbers greater than 1 that have no positive divisors other than 1 and themselves. In simpler terms, a prime number is a number that cannot be evenly divided by any other numbers except 1 and itself. Examples of prime numbers include 2, 3, 5, 7, and 11.
