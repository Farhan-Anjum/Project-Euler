Problem No.7
10001st prime

Problem URL: https://projecteuler.net/problem=7

Concept: The prime factors of a number are the prime numbers that multiply together to give that number. 
In simpler terms, prime factors are the building blocks of a number, and when you multiply them, 
you get the original number. 
For example, the prime factors of 12 are 2 and 3 because 2 * 2 * 3 equals 12.
